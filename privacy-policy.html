Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Beytona app and website (beytona.com), operated by Beytona. By using our service, you agree to this policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information, including data obtained through Google Sign-In.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Beytona, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Your profile picture (when provided by Google)
  • Your phone number (when you add it to your profile)
  • Property listing information you submit
  • Your device's IP address and usage data
  • Favorites and saved listings
  • Location data (district/village) when you search for properties

2. Google User Data — What We Access and How We Use It

Google Limited Use Compliance: Beytona's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

When you sign in to Beytona using Google Sign-In, we receive and use the following Google user data:

Data Received from Google How We Use It Where It Is Stored
Your name To display your name in your Beytona profile Supabase secure database
Your email address To create and identify your account, send important service notifications Supabase secure database
Your profile picture To display your avatar in your profile Not stored — fetched directly from Google
Google account ID To authenticate your identity securely Supabase secure database

We do not receive or access your Google password, Google contacts, Google Drive, Gmail messages, or any other Google services beyond what is listed above.

Limited Use Statement: Beytona only uses Google user data to provide and improve the Beytona real estate platform. We do not use Google user data for serving advertisements, selling to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to providing our service to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Beytona account
  • Allow you to post, manage, and browse property listings
  • Enable contact between buyers, renters, and property owners
  • Send important service notifications and updates
  • Improve and personalize your experience on the platform
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. In-App Privacy Notifications

Beytona displays privacy notifications prominently within the app interface:

  • During the sign-in process, users are informed that Beytona accesses their Google name and email address
  • When posting a listing, users are notified that their phone number will be visible to potential buyers
  • A link to this Privacy Policy is accessible from within the app at all times via the profile/settings section

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider with enterprise-grade security. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth 2.0
  • Row-level security policies on all user data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited cases:

  • With other users: Your phone number is shown to interested buyers only if you choose to post a listing
  • With service providers: Supabase (database), Google (authentication) — only as necessary to operate the platform
  • Legal requirements: When required by applicable law or to protect our legal rights

We never share Google user data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond operating the Beytona platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
  • Revoke Beytona's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@beytona.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Beytona is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at info@beytona.com.

10. Third-Party Services

Beytona uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Sign-In — Authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Database and backend. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy

11. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and maintain your session. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of the platform.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Beytona after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, our data practices, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: info@beytona.com
  • Website: legal.beytona.com
