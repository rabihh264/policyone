Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Beytona app and website (beytona.com), operated by Beytona. By using our service, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Beytona, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Your phone number (when you add it to your profile)
  • Property listing information you submit
  • Your device's IP address and usage data
  • Favorites and saved listings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Beytona platform
  • Allow you to post, manage, and browse property listings
  • Enable contact between buyers and sellers
  • Send important service notifications
  • Ensure the security of our platform

3. Google Sign-In

Beytona uses Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We do not receive your Google password. Your use of Google Sign-In is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With other users as necessary to facilitate property transactions (e.g., your phone number is shown to interested buyers if you post a listing)
  • With service providers who assist us in operating the platform (e.g., Supabase, Google)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@beytona.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Beytona is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Beytona after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@beytona.com
  • Website: www.beytona.com
